Latest Updates


This page documents the most recent changes made to the OB1 platform, including design adjustments, legal notice updates and architecture improvements. Each entry features a clear timestamp and a concise summary. These notes reflect our ongoing commitment to clarity, usability, and structural integrity.

Updates are captured daily to maintain full transparency and keep non-technical readers informed of the project’s evolution. What you see below highlights the most impactful changes over the last seven days.

A commit is the smallest recorded set of modifications in our version control system: it may be a typo fix, a CSS tweak, a legal-notice rewrite or a significant architecture refactoring. Each commit bundles all related file changes into a single logical snapshot.

To avoid overwhelming the page, we display only significant commits—those that touch multiple files, alter core architecture, update legal texts or deliver major UX/UI improvements. Smaller, purely cosmetic or single-line commits remain in the log but are omitted here for readability.

Note: The line at the bottom summarizes yesterday’s total updates and the number of files affected, giving a clear, at-a-glance overview of daily activity without listing every change.
