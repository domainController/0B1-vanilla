Early Adopter Deployment Phase

This framework is built on the conviction that any new economic model must earn its legitimacy through real-world validation — not through overextension. Unlike traditional sectors like hospitality or foodservice, whose operational dynamics are well-documented and structurally stable, this framework operates in uncharted territory. It is not an iteration; it is a structural invention.

As such, initial deployment is intentionally scoped to a limited set of early adopters — primarily within the hospitality and restaurant sectors — where operational intimacy, direct observation, and stakeholder feedback can converge into strategic refinement. This is not a sign of fragility. It is a deliberate logic of robustness: no system should scale until it masters a small circle and achieves disproportionate satisfaction within it.

All pilot interactions are Git-tracked by default, and key milestones can be Ethereum-timestamped to ensure sovereign traceability. This guarantees that strategic learnings from early deployments are sealed, reproducible, and free from narrative manipulation.

Future deployments beyond this sector are not excluded, but they are not the current operational focus. Incoming requests from other industries may be recorded, archived, or sandboxed (see: Cold Storage), but will not drive roadmap priorities.
