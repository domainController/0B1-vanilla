Platform Objective

OB1.se is a civic platform centered around a public forum, created to facilitate legal and social reflection based on user contributions. The platform may evolve into additional community tools such as OB1 Circles (e.g., WhatsApp groups, Facebook groups), always centered around the main forum.

Hosting & Infrastructure

OB1.se is currently hosted on GitHub Pages. Future hosting will be migrated to Clever Cloud. The discussion platform is being developed using Flarum, an open-source forum engine. Depending on deployment and modules, some components may change or expand.

Participation and Moderation

This platform does not offer a comment system under blog posts. The forum itself serves as the primary space for open discussion. Users are expected to engage respectfully and lawfully. All content is moderated, and harmful, defamatory, or hostile contributions will be removed.

Platform Status & Evolution

OB1 is currently in a rapid development phase. Structural, visual, and editorial changes will occur regularly, possibly several times a day. Visitors should consider the current interface as a prototype version that will evolve quickly.

Privacy & Analytics

This site will soon integrate Matomo analytics to monitor traffic and engagement anonymously. No cookies or personal identifiers will be stored without explicit consent. Data collection will comply fully with the GDPR.

Availability & Disclaimer

OB1.se is a non-commercial civic initiative. No guarantees of uptime or availability are provided. Content and structure may be modified or removed at any time.

Language Policy

The current platform is published in English to ensure international accessibility. A Swedish version will be published in due time. In case of discrepancy, the English version will prevail.
