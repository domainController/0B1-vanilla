Welcome to OB1


OB1 is a platform designed to connect individuals working off-hours and facing legal or professional challenges. It’s a space for support, solidarity, and exchange.

In order to foster collaboration and support, OB1 introduces the concept of Circles. A Circle is a way for like-minded individuals to gather based on shared interests or needs. We offer three types of circles:

  • Forum Circles – A forum hosted on OB1, organized by specific topics (coming soon).
  • Message Circles – Real-time peer exchange through messaging platforms like WhatsApp or Telegram.
  • Social Circles – Informal connections via social media platforms such as Facebook.

To facilitate this, OB1 is preparing a registration form. This form will allow you to sign up for the Forum Circles and other Circle types as they become available. While the registration form is being prepared, you can still explore OB1’s mission and get ready to join our community.
