Let's Discuss Shift Work !

Last updated: Tuesday, April 29, 2025 at 12:24 AM

OB1 is a platform designed to connect individuals working off-hours and facing legal or professional challenges. It’s a space for support, solidarity, and practical exchange.

To facilitate exchanges, OB1 suggests starting with three main types of circles:

  • Forum (Coming Soon) — Structured discussions on OB1’s forum or Slack.
  • Messaging Groups — Real-time peer exchange through platforms like WhatsApp or Telegram.
  • Social Groups — Informal connections via Facebook Groups or LinkedIn Groups.

A registration form is being prepared to allow you to join forums and messaging groups as they become available. Meanwhile, feel free to explore OB1’s manifesto and prepare to join the community.
