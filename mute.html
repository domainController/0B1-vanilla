Silent License & Narrative Suspension

This page anchors the logic of Silent License™ — a strategic artefact designed to suspend all unauthorized narrative projection, reinterpretation, or circumvention attempts. It functions not as a gag order, but as a structural lock that neutralizes secondary discourse.

Activated within the Non-Recapture Zone™, this mute layer applies to any third-party figure exposed to the framework — without co-authorship, license, or consent. It does not prevent private opinion, but enforces a public silence over core mechanisms, frameworks, and intents.

Clause — Silent License™ (D6)

Any exposure to this framework, outside a formally licensed usage or a co-authored structure, triggers a Silent License™ clause.

  • The exposed party loses the right to reinterpret or translate its contents publicly
  • No summary, paraphrasing, or derivative adaptation is authorized
  • This license activates automatically after the first full reading or redistribution
  • Silent License™ may be lifted only through explicit consent from the author, under new terms

Reminder: This artefact operates at a structural level. Any attempt to bypass it by rewriting intentions, reframing the context, or appropriating fragments will trigger disqualification of all potential engagement — commercial, legal, or reputational.
