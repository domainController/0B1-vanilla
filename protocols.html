Exposure Scenarios

Asset Scrutiny Framework™ — a strategic agency model designed to clean, assess, and reposition any overlooked asset or partnership, from inside.

Two exposure protocols exists:

  • A strategic, low-friction immersion conducted within a delimited asset or perimeter. It aims to diagnose real viability — relational, operational, narrative, and ethical — without triggering invasive tools or HR protocols.
  • A sovereign propagation strategy activating through symbolic infiltration of an integrity cell. Designed to dissolve internal toxic patterns through ethical contagion. Deployment is triggered by GP or CEO assent.
